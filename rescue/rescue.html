da orffo|Rescue Trailer is a concept of a transport module for rescue services designed to carry specialist equipment on the tough terrain, including an off-road vehicle inaccessible.



The project was initiated and developed in cooperation with the Polish Mountain Rescue Institute (PIRG), to fit the needs of GOPR services (Voluntary Mountain Rescue Organization ) during cave rescue actions, but the trailer can be used in various kinds of actions.



Division into zones, organization and clear location of equipment accelerate the response time of rescuers during the action and facilitate decision-making regarding the use of equipment.


The readiness of the set for a possible call, as well as the ability to quickly pack the equipment, shortens the time of travel to the victim and return to the base.


Units of all types of special services may have several modules configured for a given type of action, ready to be mounted interchangeably: on a trailer chassis, on skids or attached to a helicopter.


Simple construction, limitation of mechanical and electrical solutions, reduce production costs and eliminate the probability of failure.


The perforation of the internal bulkheads allows for the freedom in the load arrangement, and the folded walls of the outer shell allow for the equipment to be put “loose” in the event of a quick evacuation.


Rescue Trailer is a specialist product. It can be delivered in a universal configuration or according to the user's special assumptions.

Upper Body Features

You probably noticed what we fight for in our body concept. It’s drop-down sides that give you easy access to your equipment and working tops all around your trailer.

We just prefer to keep our stuff in the right height so we don’t need to bend down too much!



Steel constructed coffer makes the body durable and resistant to corrosion and temperature. The chest is sealed tight with replaceable rubber gaskets, which keep interior dry!



Easy to unassemble, waterproof drop-down sides go with different finish material. Choose fitting option from our offer.



Interior is made from waterproof plywood with stainless steel connectors. Dividers’ perforation works well with QuickFist’s attach system.

Feel free to replace and reconfigure your sections dividers and equipment arrangement.

It’s also designed to fit a roof tent. We use 120x160cm Autohome Overland Medium.



Whole interior is enlighten with LED lights controlled with the Power Panel.

Chassis Features

Galvanized and powder coated chassis (duplex) provides durability and resistance to damage and corrosion

Short drawbar, low frame weight and short trailer dimensions - ease of movement in the field, reversing and turning back

Place for canisters with fuel, gas cylinders, battery and water tank between wheels - direct access, lower center of gravity

Independent suspension and drawbar with stabilizer - leveling unevenness of the terrain, no bouncing effect

Equipment fastened on steel chassis guards - protection against damage

Driving Features

Frame: spatial frame, stainless, bent and welded seamless tubes - flexibility and high resistance to overload

Drawbar: adjustable - variable hitch height, fit for variable height of the hook in the vehicle

Hitch: stabilized ball hitch - the trailer is holding the track, leveling tilts in curves and in sloping terrain

Chassis: Independent suspension, longitudinal trailing arms - high ground clearance, smooth work in uneven terrain

Shock absorber: coilover shock absorbers with threaded bodies - height adjustment and softness of shock absorption

Brake: parking brake / maneuvering - safe detaching of the trailer in an inclined area

Supports: jockey wheel with lock and folded, twisted rear supports - mechanical leveling without falling

Wheels: wheel size 265/75 R16, replaceable hub - option of changing the trailer wheels on the car wheels
