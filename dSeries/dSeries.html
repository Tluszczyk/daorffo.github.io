D1 is our main product and a fulfillment of our dream, that we have been working on for past couple of years.

All of our traveling experience, design skills and market knowledge was forged into D1 to create most unique trailer to fit your overlanding needs.

Da Orffo D1 is an off-road trailer suited for European steep passages in highlands of Transylvania, narrow village streets in Pyrenees and deep muds of Montenegro.

Take a look around. If you have more questions, contact us!

You probably noticed what we fight for in our body concept. It’s drop-down sides that give you easy access to your equipment and working tops all around your trailer.

We just prefer to keep our stuff in the right height so we don’t need to bend down too much!

Camping. Utility Features

Most important feature of D1 is setting up the camp.


0. Find the perfect spot.
1. Use the handles to relocate the trailer around.

2. Pull down the Jockey wheel and level your trailer.
3. Apply the handbrake.
4. Unfold the back supports to keep it stable.
5. Set up your roof tent.
6. Open up the sides.

7. Check out each of D1’s body sections!

Kitchen Section

In 2014 on the Hungarian border traffic we couldn’t have a better opportunity to fold out the kitchen worktop, play music and prepare coffee for us and the cars around.


Kitchen section gives you 150cm worktop to feel free space while preparing any food or drinks.

90cm kitchen shelf with rubber attachments to keep the dishes, cutlery, cutting board, bottles, boxes and other stuff at your fingertips.

Place for a double torch gas stove with gas installation underneath the shelves.

Easy access to a fridge on a sliding supports.

On demand, we will install sink with a tap.

Tools Section
We will need an axe! …and a shovel!


Quick access to the tools is a must!
Why not to have them all in the sight?

Customize your Tools Section to your needs. Fishing equipment? Hunting riffles? Tripod with a cooking pot? Yes! Let’s fit them there too!

And this face of security guards every time we open this side for border control. :D

Inventory Section


120x70x50cm space for bigger stuff you don’t want to worry about with access from the front and from the kitchen side.

We keep here the fridge on the sliding supports, but when we decide to take the fridge to the car we exchange it for a storage boxes.

Electrical installation keeps the fridge connected to the power.

Bottled water, additional tents and bags can go there as well.

Baggage Section
…the last but not least.


Two sliding drawers for loose equipment like shoes, clothes, cooking pots and paper towel.

Large shelf for camping table and 4 folding chairs with a belt attachment.

Power Control Panel to control battery status, lights inside and outside the trailer and couple power plugs.

Small storage space at your service. We like to fit there small extinguisher and a camping flashlight.

Chassis Features

Galvanized and powder coated chassis (duplex) provides durability and resistance to damage and corrosion

Short drawbar, low frame weight and short trailer dimensions - ease of movement in the field, reversing and turning back

Place for canisters with fuel, gas cylinders, battery and water tank between wheels - direct access, lower center of gravity

Independent suspension and drawbar with stabilizer - leveling unevenness of the terrain, no bouncing effect

Equipment fastened on steel chassis guards - protection against damage

Driving Features

Frame: spatial frame, stainless, bent and welded seamless tubes - flexibility and high resistance to overload

Drawbar: adjustable - variable hitch height, fit for variable height of the hook in the vehicle

Hitch: stabilized ball hitch - the trailer is holding the track, leveling tilts in curves and in sloping terrain

Chassis: Independent suspension, longitudinal trailing arms - high ground clearance, smooth work in uneven terrain

Shock absorber: coilover shock absorbers with threaded bodies - height adjustment and softness of shock absorption

Brake: parking brake / maneuvering - safe detaching of the trailer in an inclined area

Supports: jockey wheel with lock and folded, twisted rear supports - mechanical leveling without falling

Wheels: wheel size 265/75 R16, replaceable hub - option of changing the trailer wheels on the car wheels
